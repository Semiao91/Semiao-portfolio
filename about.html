Where it all began

Coming all the way from Portugal I had my first contact with coding when I was 15 years old, that was when i discovered Unity, an engine tailored for game development. I spent some time to learn C# and learned to code a few basic side scrollers.

Education

I then attended UALG college in order to pursue a carrier in CS , however due to my financial situation and emotional state at the time I was forced to quit and had to start working in order to sustain myself.

Work

A few years later I rejoined university and completed my bachelors in Business administration which landed my first corporate job working at Apple as a technical advisor. This then led to an offer for a sales position in Germany that would kickstart my career in corporate sales for 3 years, eventually ending my last sales job in TeamViewer.

Currently

Due to lack of interest in the previous work field I have decided to commit myself to learning to code everyday in order to pursue a long time passion of Coding. I am currently doing a front end mentorship program at Brainnest consulting in order to further my techncal capabilities.
